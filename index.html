About

Watson Woodwork was set up to create a range of bespoke products to suit any client's needs. I am keen to combine woodcarving, sculpture, bronze work and gilding skills creatively to produce top quality and unique products that can be tailored to the customer's individual requirements.

I am a highly qualified craftsman working alongside a team of colleagues, enthusiastic about working in both traditional and modern styles, using expertise gained over several years of training at City and Guilds London Art School.

I am happy to work along side my clients to ensure that we can produce a first class piece of work that will be an investment for the future and will always be 'one of a kind'.

If you are interested in my services at Watson Woodwork or have any questions, feel free to get in touch. I look forward to hearing from you!
